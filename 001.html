Cadence Annals didn't begin as a conceptually cohesive project, but, rather, grew piecemeal out of the collaborations, transitions, work, and travel that have dominated my life over the past couple of years. This busy and fruitful time left me with plenty of source material, though not much room for recording. So, I chipped away at various notions and followed an assortment of hunches, the result being an aggregate of ideas and experiences rendered in synths, guitars, horns, and field recordings. 33' cassette, edition of 50.
